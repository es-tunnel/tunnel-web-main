## From the mind of Shane Black

***The Nice Guys*** is a 2016 American [neo-noir](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neo-noir) [buddy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buddy_film) [action comedy film](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Action_comedy_film) directed by [Shane Black](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shane_Black), who co-wrote the screenplay with Anthony Bagarozzi. The film stars [Russell Crowe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russell_Crowe) and [Ryan Gosling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ryan_Gosling) in the title roles, with [Angourie Rice](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angourie_Rice), [Matt Bomer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matt_Bomer), [Margaret Qualley](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Margaret_Qualley), [Keith David](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keith_David), and [Kim Basinger](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kim_Basinger) appearing in supporting roles. Set in 1977 [Los Angeles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Los_Angeles), the film focuses on [private eye](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Private_investigator) Holland March (Gosling) and tough [enforcer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mob_enforcer) for hire Jackson Healy (Crowe) who team up to investigate the disappearance of a teenage girl (Qualley).

_The Nice Guys_ premiered on May 11, 2016, in [Hollywood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hollywood,_Los_Angeles), screened on May 15 at the [2016 Cannes Film Festival](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2016_Cannes_Film_Festival), and was released by [Warner Bros. Pictures](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Warner_Bros._Pictures) in the United States on May 20, 2016. It received positive reviews from critics for its humor, mystery, and the performances of Crowe and Gosling. It grossed $62 million on a $50 million budget.[[6]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Nice_Guys#cite_note-reviews-6)
